# Outreach plan — September launch

**Goal:** 6–8 people at the first session.
**Timeline:** now to 16 September 2026.

---

## Pools of people to reach

- **Direct network** — colleagues, former colleagues, friends who work in data/tech/adjacent fields
- **Warm connections** — people met at events (WiDS, DataAfterwork, etc.) you're loosely in touch with
- **Connectors** — people who might not come themselves but know someone (use Version 3 invite)
- **Cold reach** — LinkedIn followers who engaged with your post, or people in relevant communities

---

## Rhythm (now → September)

### May–June: personal invites
- Send 2–3 WhatsApp invites per week to direct network
- Follow up with anyone who didn't reply after 1 week (one nudge only)
- Identify 3–5 connectors and send them the "do you know anyone?" version

### July: broaden
- Post a reminder or update on LinkedIn (personal) — e.g. "first session coming up, still a few spots"
- Ask the one confirmed yes if they know anyone
- Check if any relevant communities or Slack groups exist (WiDS Geneva, local data meetups)

### August: confirm and close
- Email subscribers via Buttondown with a "see you in September" note
- Resend announcement to any new subscribers who joined after the first send
- Check RSVP form responses and follow up with maybes

### Early September: final push
- One last LinkedIn post — "one week away, still time to join"
- Send reminder email via Buttondown (~12 September)
- Manually resend announcement to any new subscribers (Buttondown automations require paid plan)

---

## Things to track
- [ ] Subscriber count (check Buttondown weekly)
- [ ] RSVP form responses (check weekly from August)
- [ ] Who you've invited — keep a simple list so you don't lose track or double-message

---

## Notes
- Don't invite people out of obligation — invite people you'd actually want in the room
- A specific personal message ("I thought of you") converts better than a mass forward
- Quality over quantity — 6 engaged people beats 12 half-committed ones
