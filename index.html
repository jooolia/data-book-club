Data Book Club Morges, Switzerland — every two months

A small book club in the Morges area for people working in data, tech, and AI. One book every two months — accessible but rigorous, connecting technology to real life, society, and ethics. Good conversation. No pressure to finish every chapter.

Format

  • Morges — location being finalised
