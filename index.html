Data Book Club Morges, Switzerland — quarterly

A small book club in the Morges area for people working in data, tech, and AI. One book per season — accessible but rigorous, connecting technology to real life, society, and ethics. Good conversation. No pressure to finish every chapter.

Format

  • Quarterly sessions — one book per season
  • Weeknight evenings, Morges
  • 6–8 people, intimate by design
  • No commitment — join any session
  • Language: English
