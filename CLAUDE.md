# Data Book Club — Project Context

## What this is
A small, quarterly book club in Morges focused on data, devops, and AI in society. Books that are discussable and opinion-generating, not purely technical. Inspired by Brandi Beals' Data Book Club model.

**Editorial line:** accessible but rigorous, connecting data and tech to real life, society, ethics, and work experience. Not textbooks.

**Audience:** open to all, drawing from the Lausanne–Geneva corridor — international, multilingual professionals interested in data, tech, and AI in society.

## Key decisions made
- Quarterly (4 books/year)
- Weeknight evening sessions
- Small group: 6–8 to start
- No commitment required, join any session
- Venue: Central Station Morges (visited, confirmed good vibe)
