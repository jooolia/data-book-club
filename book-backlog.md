# Book backlog

Ideas for future sessions — bring to the group if no one has a suggestion.

## Shortlist
- *Invisible Women* — Caroline Criado Perez. Data bias against women, personally relevant, very discussable.
- *Weapons of Math Destruction* — Cathy O'Neil. Algorithms causing harm, clear argument, accessible.
- *Atlas of AI* — Kate Crawford. Power and politics behind AI. More challenging but rich.
- *Noise* — Kahneman, Sunstein & Sibony. Why human judgment is inconsistent. Great for data people.
- *The Intelligence Trap* — David Robson. Why smart people make bad decisions. Universally relatable.
